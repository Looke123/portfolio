About

I'm a music composer and sound designer based in Milan, with a background spanning performance, academia, and industry. I studied at NAM Milano and graduated — both bachelor's and master's — from the Civica Scuola di Musica Claudio Abbado. My work sits at the intersection of emotion and precision, where every note and texture serves the story.

From scoring award-winning short films to designing soundscapes for theatre productions, advertising campaigns, and branded content, I bring a versatile approach grounded in deep collaboration. I believe great sound doesn't just accompany visuals — it transforms them.

Whether you need an original score, bespoke sound design, or a complete audio identity for your brand, I'm here to bring your vision to life.

Sound Timeline

A journey through the key moments that shaped my practice — from academic formation to professional collaborations in film, theatre, advertising, and live sound.

2017 – 2019

NAM — Nuova Accademia Musicale, Milano

Formazione come chitarrista e performer, con focus su performance live e studio. Un percorso che ha posto le basi del mio approccio alla musica applicata, tra tecnica strumentale, improvvisazione e lavoro d'insieme.

2019 – 2024

Civica Scuola di Musica Claudio Abbado — Laurea Triennale + Magistrale

Percorso accademico completo in composizione e sound design presso una delle più importanti scuole di musica italiane. Cinque anni di ricerca, produzione e approfondimento teorico che hanno definito il mio linguaggio sonoro.

2020

Esperienze nel Cinema — CISA & CSM

Inizio delle collaborazioni nell'ambito cinematografico attraverso rapporti con CISA e CSM. Compositore per il cortometraggio "Per Finta" (2025, regia Diego Fossati) — vincitore di diversi premi internazionali.

2022

Teatro & Performing Arts

Esperienze nell'ambito teatrale e performativo, culminate nello spettacolo "Diari sotto alle bombe: Bisogna saper uccidere quando è necessario" (2024). FOH & Tecnico Live — Rassegne Milanesi

Attività come FOH engineer e tecnico del suono per diverse rassegne musicali milanesi. Un'esperienza sul campo che ha affinato la percezione dello spazio sonoro e la gestione in tempo reale del mix live.

2023

Inizio dell'attività freelance come compositore e sound designer nell'ambito pubblicitario. Collaborazioni con le agenzie Adverteam, Jackleg e Imaze per la produzione di contenuti audio per campagne nazionali e digitali.

Services

From brief to final delivery, end-to-end audio solutions tailored to your creative vision and business objectives.

Music for Advertising & Branded Content

Original compositions that capture your brand's essence and connect emotionally with your audience.

  • Original music tailored to your brand voice and campaign goals
  • Delivery optimized for TV, cinema, web, and social formats
  • Quick turnaround for tight production schedules

Original Score for Film & Theatre

Narrative-driven music that serves the story and amplifies emotional impact. Close collaboration with directors from concept through final dub.

  • Thematic scoring that evolves with your narrative arc
  • Spotting sessions and iterative creative process
  • Full orchestration or hybrid electronic/acoustic palettes

Sound Design & Audio Post

Every sonic detail crafted to immerse your audience. Sound design that elevates your visual content.

  • Custom Foley, atmospheres, and effect design
  • Dialogue editing and ADR supervision
  • Final mix in stereo, 5.1, or Dolby Atmos

Audio Branding & Sonic Logos

Strategic audio identities that make your brand instantly recognizable across every touchpoint.

  • Sonic logo design with flexible adaptations
  • UI/UX sound design for apps and digital products
  • Brand audio guidelines and implementation support

Live Sound (FOH)

FOH engineering and live sound management for concerts, festivals, and performing arts events in Milan and across Italy.

  • Front-of-house mixing for live performances
  • Stage monitoring and technical setup
  • Experience with multiple venues and formats

Music Supervision & Licensing

Expert guidance on music selection, rights clearance, and original vs. licensed music strategy.

  • Strategic music selection aligned with creative vision
  • License negotiation and rights management
  • Custom cover versions and re-records when needed

Clients

Trusted by brands, agencies, production companies, and cultural institutions.
